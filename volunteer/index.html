Whether you can give an hour or a weekend, your time makes a real difference in Kansas communities. No special skills required — just a willingness to help.

Volunteer Opportunities

Property Rehabilitation

Help with painting, cleaning, landscaping, and light construction. First Saturday of each month. All skill levels welcome.

Event Support

Help organize and run community events — setup, registration, food service, and cleanup.

Administrative & Outreach

Data entry, grant research, social media, photography, or community outreach. Work from home available.

Skilled Services (Pro Bono)

Licensed electricians, plumbers, attorneys, accountants, and contractors — your expertise is invaluable and tax-deductible.
